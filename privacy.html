Last updated: 10 July 2026

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your enquiry and, where relevant, to provide the travel or business advisory services you have requested. We do not sell or rent your information to third parties.

How we protect it

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect any information you share with us. Enquiries submitted through this site are sent directly to our concierge team's email address.

Your rights

You may ask us at any time what information we hold about you, request its correction, or ask us to delete it. Contact info@resplendentglobaltravel.com for any of these requests.

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date above.
